About Me

Dr. Ritu Kela is a practising Psychologist with an experience of 19 years. She is located in Meerut. Dr. Ritu Kela practices at the Darpan From Dr. Ritu Kela in Meerut. The Darpan From Dr. Ritu Kela is situated at 49, Chanakya Puri, Shastri Nagar, Meerut. She pursued her MSc in the year 1996 from The MS University of Baroda. She completed her PhD in the year 2006 from CCS University, Meerut. She has also done her Certificate Course in Psychology in the year 2018 from University Of Liverpool England. Dr. Ritu Kela is an experienced, skilled and awarded doctor in her field of specialization. She worked at
